Teaching AI to Understand Us

We break down human communication into simple pieces that AI can digest and learn from.

01

Reading & Writing

We train AI to read our books, news, and everyday conversations. We teach it the grammar, the meaning behind sentences, and how to answer questions correctly.

02

Listening & Speaking

We help AI listen to our voices. By capturing local community voices, we teach it to understand urban and rural accents across Pakistan.

03

Seeing the World

We connect images of our local environment with Urdu descriptions. This allows AI to "see" Pakistan and describe it accurately.
